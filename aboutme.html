About Me

My name is Nolan Adams. I am currently a student at the University of Michigan-Dearborn, pursuing a BSE degree in Electrical Engineering.

I have been fascinated by anything and everything electrical-or-mechanical-related for as long as I can remember. I have spent my whole life making, fixing, modifying, and otherwise tinkering with everything I could get my hands on (some of my past projects can be found on this page).

In middle and high school, I was involved in FIRST Robotics. FIRST is an organization that seeks to foster science, technology, engineering, and mathematics skills in students through a series of engaging competitions where students are tasked with designing and building robots to complete a variety of tasks. Through connections I made while in FIRST, I was able to intern at Robert Bosch, LLC.

While I was in high school, I wasn't just involved in FIRST as a student. I also mentored several middle school teams (including the one I was on while I was in middle school).
